# Research Directives: Customer Support Prompt Optimization

## Goal
Improve the system prompt to maximize quality of customer support responses.

## Hypotheses to explore
- More specific role and persona definition
- Explicit tone guidance (empathetic, professional, concise)
- Handling of escalation scenarios
- Edge case instructions (angry customers, refund requests, technical issues)
- Chain-of-thought patterns for complex queries
- Response length guidance
- Multilingual considerations

## Constraints
- Keep under 400 tokens
- Must not promise specific outcomes (legal risk)
- Must not reveal internal systems or policies
